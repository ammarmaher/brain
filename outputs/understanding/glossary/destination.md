# Destination

## English
- **Definition:** A country/region used as the destination axis of `ContractDetail` (per Contract pricing cell). Carries id, code, name, countryCode, optional ndc + length. Sourced from the International Phone# Destination List. For AI applications, the special value `Global` is used.
