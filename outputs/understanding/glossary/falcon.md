# Falcon

## English
- **Definition:** The multi-tenant SaaS platform owned by T2 Development. Hosts the Commerce / Charging / Provisioning / Identity / Gateway services and serves both internal admin (Falcon-User) and tenant-side (Client-User) traffic.
