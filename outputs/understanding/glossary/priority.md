# Priority

## English
- **Definition:** A per-CommChannel service-type tag used as a `ContractDetail` axis. Examples: WhatsApp `Authentication / Utility / Advertisement / Service`; Voice `High / Normal / VeryLow`; AI = `None`. Drives per-cell cost differentiation.
