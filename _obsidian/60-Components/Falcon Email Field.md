# Falcon Email Field

> Email input pairing a native `<input type="email">` with an optional in-field "Verify" button — both sharing one outer border (single-element look). Consumer triggers verification on demand via the `falcon-verify` event. For plain email (no verify), `<falcon-angular-input type="email">` is sufficient.
