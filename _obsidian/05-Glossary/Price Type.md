# Price Type

## English
- **Definition:** The pricing cadence on a `CommChannelConfig` or `AppConfig`. Enum: `Monthly | Yearly | One Time Payment`. Drives renewal date arithmetic and grace-period length (Monthly = 7 days; Yearly / OneTime = 30 days).
