# Account Settings

## English
- **Definition:** Per-Account security + limits config bundle. Carries `passwordSecurityLevel`, `allowedIps[]`, `maxNormalUserLimit`, `maxSystemUserLimit`, `maxNodeLevels`, and `balanceTransferLimitPct`. Configured at Step 2 of the Add Client wizard; edited later via the Organization Hierarchy Settings tab.
