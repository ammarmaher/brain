# Role

## English
- **Definition:** A User's job assignment. Backend enum `eUserRoles` keyed by **RoleKey** strings: `sys-admin | operation | product | account-owner | node-admin | normal-user`. Canonical id = RoleKey (per `UserRolePolicy.GetRoleFromRoleKey`).
