# Balance Type

## English
- **Definition:** Half of the per-Account wallet-topology configuration. Enum: `User | Node`. Determines whether balances are held by Users or Nodes. Combined with [[Wallet Type]] yields the 2×2 wallet topology matrix. Configurable by Falcon usertype only.
