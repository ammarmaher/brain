# Transfer

## English
- **Definition:** Moving SAR balance between two wallets within an Account's topology. Entity: `TransferTx`. Allowed source-×-destination pairs follow a strict matrix (Master ↔ Comm, Comm ↔ User/Node, User ↔ User). Subject to [[Account Limitations]] balance-transfer-limit-percent.
