# Contact Group

## English
- **Definition:** A named list of recipients owned by a single creator, stored under a specific Node in the hierarchy. Status: `In Progress | Completed`. Carries `softDeleted` flag (independent of status).

## Arabic
- **Term:** **مجموعة جهات الاتصال** (per imported-business `multilanguage-rules.md`).
- **Definition:** Not captured in available sources — only the term name is documented. Surface gap.
