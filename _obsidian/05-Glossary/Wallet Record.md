# Wallet Record

## English
- **Definition:** An atomic credit record inside a Wallet, tagged with a specific Contract. The load-bearing entity for contract linkage. Survives Contract `Expired` (records persist) but is excluded from Master Wallet lump-sums. Nearest-expiring traversal unit on deduction.
