# Addon

## English
- **Definition:** A sub-service attached to a Contract with a free-credit bucket and a rate card. Targets a `subServiceType` (e.g. `VoiceNumber`, `NabaaTemplate`). Maps to `ContractQuotaRequest` + `ContractOverageRateRequest`.
