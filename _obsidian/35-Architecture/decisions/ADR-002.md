# ADR-002 — Why Tailwind v4 instead of SCSS

> **Status:** accepted · **Reversal cost:** high · **Decided:** 2026-05-16

## TL;DR

Tailwind v4 utility classes on Angular templates only. The `@theme` block in `libs/falcon-theme/src/falcon-tailwind-tokens.css` is the SoT (218 tokens). No SCSS, no inline styles, no component CSS. Per-component `*.tokens.css` companion files merge with the canonical theme.

**Verification:** 35 R-FE-002 violations remain (down from 44 after this morning's PATTERN-04 sweep), 13 documented blockers for theme-curator pass.
